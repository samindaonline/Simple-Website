Introducing Wing Shoe: Crafting Elegance, Step by Step

Welcome to Wing Shoe, where every step is a testament to artistry and passion. Nestled in the quaint town of Millawitiya, Kuruwita, our small shoe manufacturing institute has been weaving dreams into reality since 1994. Founded by the dynamic duo of Vinitha Nalani and Eric Perera, Wing Shoe has carved a niche for itself in the world of handcrafted footwear.

Our journey began with a vision to create exquisite handmade ladies' shoes that exuded grace, elegance, and uncompromising quality. Drawing inspiration from the rich heritage of our island, we sought to infuse traditional craftsmanship with contemporary designs, resulting in timeless pieces that truly make a statement.

Whether you're attending a special occasion, striding confidently into the boardroom, or simply seeking everyday comfort, Wing Shoe has a perfect fit for every occasion. Immerse yourself in the elegance of our creations and let us accompany you on your journey, one step at a time.
